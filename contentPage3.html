Overview

Circular economy is a forward looking approach to the economic development which will increase the sustainability of consumption and production. This concept ensures a future with geenary as it contributes to protect the environment. The key concept of circular economy is to increase business opportunities while eliminating the environmental pollution.
This concepts differs from the traditional linear economy. In linear economy, we use a method called take, make and waste. But in circular economy we use the reduce, reuse and recycle method. There are numerous advantages that we can gain by adopting the circular economy concepts. Nowadays, most of the reputable companies are considering adopting this approach.
This page is dedicated to make you familiar with the circular economy concept which is a part of responsible consumption and production, the 12 th sustainable development goal of united nations

How Circular Economy works

Circular economy reduces the usage of raw materials by keeping the products in use for a long period of time. If a product is broken and can be used after repairing, in circular economy the lifetime of that product will be extended by repairing them. If the product is no longer usable, a new product will be made out of it instead of considering it as waste.
Product Design
Design products which will long last
Design products which can reasily be repaired and reused
Reuse and Repair
Encourage people to reuse the products under circular economy
Provide services to repair the products for the reusage of them
Create New Products
Create new products by using the raw materials of the products which cannot be resued anymore
Contribute to the economical growth by selling these products

Advantages of Circular Economy

Reduces the environmental pollution

Increases the usage of renewable materials

Real World Examples

H&M
H&M is one of the most popular companies the fashion world. It has started a venture called Looper Textile Co, where the company collects unwanted clothes and provides them to the second hand fashion companies. This will give a new life to the unwanted clothes while reduing the waste. This is also an obvious example for the circular economy
IKEA
IKEA gives the opportunity for thier customers to sell their used IKEA furniture to the company and the company will exchange an IKEA refund card for that. This shows how this company uses the circular economy concept effectively

How Can You Contribute

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
Reduce the usage of disposable products in day to day life. Try to use products that you can reuse as long as possible and make new products wtih reusable materials
Repair the broken items
Don't try to buy a new item from a shop whenever something is broken. First think if the item can be reused by repairing it. If so, avoid buying new items and creating unneccesary waste
